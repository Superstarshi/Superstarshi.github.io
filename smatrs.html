About

--------------------------------------

Super Mario and the Rainbow Stars is a project that aims to bring the unique traits of the Paper Mario and Mario & Luigi series into a fun 2D platformer. Explore 7 different chapters, discover new partners and unique worlds, and stop Lord Dark Star's evil plans! Features: new moves, cutscenes, 3D world map, tons of powerups, a 3 hp life system, partner system, original songs and more!

Story

--------------------------------------

One day, the Mario Bros were hanging out near their house until they suddenly received a letter from Peach. They've been invited for the Star Festival! Before that festival starts, they're planning to take a trip with a hot air balloon. Things turn out for the worse once something goes wrong...

Everyone gets scattered, and now it's up to Mario to find his friends, as well as retrieve the Rainbow Stars, who were scattered by the newly awakened Dark Star. Mario must get these Rainbow Stars before the Dark Star, otherwise the plan of plunging the world into darkness might succeed... Thankfully Mario will be accompanied by many different partners alongside his journey!
