Changelog

v1.0.1 - 17/8/2024

Changes:

Added an ''assist'' mode, which makes Mario's max HP 6, instead of 3.

Added a ''daredevil'' mode, which sets Mario's HP to always be at 1.

Added a ''carefree'' mode, for if you just want to play through the levels without worry.

Made the Goombooter boss fight easier.

The screen now darkens when you unfocus the window, rather than displaying a ''Pause'' text.

In the secret level, the time left after beating said level now displays milliseconds.

Made one part easier in the latter half of 1-6.

Tweaked the second section in 1-7 to make it a bit easier.

Made the dolphin ride section in 1-7 a little easier, and made it more clear how the controls work by talking to the Yoshi at the start.

Slightly adjusted the volume of the level unlock sound.

Bug Fixes:

Fixed a bug where during the partner tutorial, Mario would sometimes unintentionally face the opposite direction, making it impossible to continue.

Fixed a bug where the giant bomb sound during Goombooter's boss fight would continue to play after the boss was defeated.

Fixed a bug where if you collected the Rainbow Star too quickly at the end of 1-7, it would cause the game to softlock.

The star effect now stops immediately after getting a Rainbow Star.
