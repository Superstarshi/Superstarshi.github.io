FAQ

Won't Nintendo take this game down?

Nintendo only takes down fangames that make a profit or are remakes of existing games. Besides, SMBX has existed for many years and has never once been taken down.

Is multiplayer supported?

Currently no, but there may be plans in the future to support it.

Are multiple languages supported?

Right now it's just English, but more languages will be supported in future updates.

Is Mario the only playable character?

Yes. But the game does have segments where you play as others, akin to Paper Mario.

When is the full game releasing?

When it's ready.
